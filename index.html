Our Goal

War may not allow even the most developped countries to deliver timely measures to the military supply requirements. Combat situation frequently puts unexpected tasks and requires supplies that are hard to predict at any level of military commandment. It is not a secret that in combat situations equipment deteriorates fast, gets destroyed or lost. This leads to the fact that country cannot always support all the troop requirements at the front lines. Large charitable foundations as a rule focus on strategic needs, such as unmanned aerial vehicles and recoinasanse satellites. On the other hand, small charitable foundations that are in direct contact with particular divisions possess information about the needs of soldiers themselves at the front lines. They are capable of promptly accumulating foundations and utilizing them for everyday needs of soldiers. Such needs include the repair and maintanance of machinery, provision of communication equipment, optics, heating means. At the present moment there is a number of such small foundations created by conscious and caring citizens of Ukraine. They are all needed and do their job well. They all contribute to the victory of humanity over the beastly rage of the aggressor. The realities of our time are that we must protect the independency of Ukraine by all possible means. And with it, we protect the staiblity in Europe and the whole world. Due to this exact reason we have decided to create the charitable foundation "Strength in Unity" that puts effort to support the 5th separate regiment and 204th separate batalion of territorial defence, that are fighting hard battles with the Russian aggressor in the eastern Ukraine in Donetsk region right now. The mentioned divisions are suffering heavy losses in this brutal war. Our goal is to help them win and stay alive. Even before this foundation was created, together with our friends at our own expense we purchased and handed over 50 bulletproof vests, more than 5000 canned foods, a medical vehicle and military fortification materials. Right now, the purchase of electrical generators and Starlink 3G modems is under consideration. Attracting funds from Ukrainians and from a wider circle of interested parties beyond the Ukrainian borders, will lead our assistance to a new level and will make a much more significant contribution to the important cause set by the Armed Forces of Ukraine and the entire Ukrainian people! Thank you for supporting us! Glory to Ukraine. Glory to the Armed forces of Ukraine!

About Us

We are Ukrainian lawyers Oleksandr Kobylyansyi and Pavlo Livochka. In 2019 we founded a Legal Association and developed our business in the Kyiv city. But after the beginning of the full-scale Russian military agression on 24th of February 2022 our life like the lifes of everyone in Ukraine suffered shocking changes. Russian world mislead by the idea of their supremacy came to our land. It came with missiles, artilery and tanks. It came bringing death and destruction, killing children and raping women, demolishing civillian infrastructure, erasing entire cities, planting their values, culture and language. Their leaders deny the right of Ukrainains to be an independent nation. Controlled by them media are calling to kill Ukrainian children. In the XXIst century Europe faces with an open genocide of millions of the Ukrainian people. At the age of five, our children already clearly tell apart the life before war and after. It is impossible to run away and hide from the Russian hordes. It's useless to appease. They do not care about distance and state borders. They do not value the desire of people to live independently and worthily. The only choice that is left is the fight for our freedom. The only solution is to kick them back with such strength that they forget about their dreams of supremacy and subjugation of other nations. At this point the existance of the Ukrainian nation is under question. Our goal is to participate in the resistance leaded by the Ukrainian Armed Forces. From the start Kobylyanskyi Oleksandr took up volunteering activity. Pavlo Livochka without hesitation joined the territorial defences of Kyiv on the first day of the Russian offence. Having military experience from 2014 he participated in battles for the defence of Kyiv and liberation of Kyiv region as part of the 204th separate batallion. He took part in the battles for the village of Moshchun where lots of enemy efforts were concentrated in attempt to break through to the Ukrainian capital. Now we decided to combine our efforts and create the charitable foundation "Stronger Together" that focuses on the support of 5th separate assault regiment and 204th separate territorial defense batallion of the Armed Forces of Ukraine in which our brothers are serving, and are currently fighting hard battles aggainst the Russian aggressor in the Donetsk region in eastern Ukraine.
